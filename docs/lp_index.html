About Team

A platform that supports a community growth and people to help each other

Think about how long it is to find someone to take care your pets during holidays season? Or, how helpful it would be when we do grocery for our grandparents once in a while?

VoxBox is a social platform that is aimed to connect volunteers and people who needs some help within a community.

Why Volunteer?

Volunteering within a community is a natural way to meet contacts and know our surrounding. Volunteering is also a great example of compassion, solidarity and responsibility - HelpGuide

ABOUT TEAM

VoxBox is made by students in HackYourFuture Belgium as a part of final assignment after following eight months of a solid coding course. This project is inspired by daily difficulties faced both in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic and in any other regular days. The team hopes that providing help could be a part of our daily activities as social beings. (Check out our Linkedin to connect with the member of the team!)
